About Our Non Profit - NGO

Our Vision

The main aims and objectives of the Foundation are:

  • - To work towards the socio-economic upliftment and development of weaker/ underprivileged section of the societies of our country with special focus on tribal areas, women and children, development of model villages etc.
  • - To work towards economic empowerment and leadership development of women through promoting education, entrepreneurship, self-help groups, farming etc
  • - To help underprivileged section of the society with programs for promoting health & hygiene such as providing food, clean water, medical assistance, sanitation facilities, sexually transmitted diseases;
  • - To work towards stopping all forms of gender-based violence and reducing gender inequality by helping children with education, mentorship, life-skills, vocational skills, provide camps for legal awareness etc.;
  • - To conduct surveys and promote community development programs. Provide awareness about important social/cultural/environmental issues, cyber security, online fraud education to general public; and
  • - To develop, promote, support, and maintain various activities like Handicrafts, agriculture/horticulture, Farming.
